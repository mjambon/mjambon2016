Significant achievements

Author of SuMo: a patented bioinformatics system for relevant and fast search of functional similarities in protein structures. The system was first developed as an academic research project and is now developed and distributed by MEDIT. SuMo became the first commercial biopharmaceutical software to be written in OCaml. It continues to be available to the academic community as a web service.
